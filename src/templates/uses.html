Hardware Setup

Primary Equipment

  • My primary monitor is a Dell 2417DG 24" 2560 X 1440 with two ASUS VE228H 21.5" 1920x1080 flanking it. I also have a Visio 55" mounted above my main monitor that I use for gaming or a 4th screen.
  • I'm a big fan of mechanical keyboards I use a Code Keyboard 60% at work and a CM Quickfire Stealth 80% for home.
  • I've used a Razer DeathAdder mouse with a mouse bungie to avoid tangles.
  • I switch between a Sennheiser PC 360 Headset and a SteelSeries Arctis 7 Headset for audio.
  • When screen casting, video conferencing, or streaming, I use a Logitech C920 for my video and a Blue Yeti for my microphone.
  • I work from home a lot and I use a USB Switch Hub, several USB graphics cards, and a combination of HDMI switches to switch all my monitors and input devices to my work laptop. This allows me to use my home monitors, keyboard, mouse, etc with my work laptop without needing to unplug anything. This could be solved with a KVM switch, but one that supports triple monitors is way too expensive for me.
